- **Cloud Misconfigurations:** Unsecured AWS S3 buckets and Azure Blob Storage leaking sensitive data.

#### **4. Mitigation Strategies**
- **Defensive Measures:**

  - Train employees on AI-generated phishing tactics and vishing scams.

  - Regularly update security policies and conduct red team exercises.
